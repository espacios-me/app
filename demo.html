Transformation Roadmap for Acme Realty

Transform Acme Realty from manual lead management to an automated conversion machine. This 5-phase roadmap focuses on capturing high-intent leads through Meta ads, automating WhatsApp responses with AI, and building a CRM infrastructure that turns prospects into closed deals with minimal manual intervention.

Roadmap Execution Phases

01
✓

Lead Capture Infrastructure

First, we establish your demand generation engine. We'll launch targeted Meta ad campaigns that drive qualified leads directly to WhatsApp. The automation logic routes conversations based on property interest and urgency, ensuring every lead gets instant acknowledgment while critical inquiries reach your team immediately.

02

AI Response System

Next, we deploy AI copilots to handle first-touch responses. When someone asks about a listing at 11 PM, they get instant answers about price, square footage, and neighborhood details. The AI qualifies intent by asking about timeline and budget, then escalates serious buyers to your team with complete context.

03

Shared Inbox Architecture

We then build a shared inbox that gives your entire team visibility into every conversation. No more leads getting lost because someone went on vacation. Assignment rules automatically distribute new chats, and if a message sits unanswered for 10 minutes, it escalates to a backup agent.

04

CRM Integration & Pipeline

Every WhatsApp conversation automatically creates or updates a contact in your CRM. When a lead says "I want to schedule a viewing," it triggers a calendar invite and moves them to your "Scheduled Showings" pipeline stage. Your team sees the full conversation history, property interests, and engagement score in one place.

05

Follow-up Automation & Nurture

Finally, we implement nurture sequences for leads who aren't ready to buy today. If someone expressed interest but hasn't responded in 3 days, they get a personalized WhatsApp message with new listings matching their criteria. Cold leads receive monthly market updates and property alerts, keeping you top-of-mind until they're ready to move.
