Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025 · Espacios messaging ops platform

Overview

espacios ("we", "us", "our") provides AI messaging operations and lead qualification infrastructure. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Espacios platform or engage with our services.

What we collect

We collect information you provide when you:

  • Request a quote or onboard as a client
  • Configure messaging workflows, qualification rules, or routing logic
  • Connect a WhatsApp Business number or CRM integration
  • Communicate with us via WhatsApp, email, or support channels

We also collect operational data automatically — usage patterns, device information, IP addresses, and platform logs needed to deliver the service.

Lead and conversation data

When your AI qualification system is active on WhatsApp, conversation data (messages, lead details, qualification responses) is processed through our platform. This data is used solely to power your qualification engine, populate your CRM, and generate your reporting.

We do not sell, rent, or share conversation data with third parties outside of the integrations you have explicitly configured.

How we use your information

  • Operate, deliver, and improve the Espacios platform and service
  • Run your messaging workflows, qualification flows, and routing logic
  • Sync qualified lead data to your connected CRM systems
  • Provide reporting, analytics, and performance insights
  • Communicate about your account, updates, and support matters
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

CRM and third-party integrations

Espacios may process data through CRM systems, WhatsApp providers, ad platforms, and automation tools you connect or that are required for delivery. Those platforms have their own privacy terms and policies that govern their use of your data.

Data retention

We retain data only as long as reasonably needed for service delivery, reporting, support, and compliance. If you close your account, we will delete or anonymize your data within 90 days, unless a longer period is required by law or agreed in writing.

Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share it with:

  • Service providers who assist in operating the platform (hosting, analytics, support tools)
  • CRM and integration partners you connect explicitly through Espacios
  • Law enforcement when required by applicable law

Security

We implement commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@espacios.me.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised date. Continued use of Espacios services after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at privacy@espacios.me, message us on WhatsApp at +971 52 370 9989, or visit espacios.me.
